Privacy & Cookies

This page explains, in plain language, what happens to your data when you use this website and when you send a booking request.

What we collect

When you submit the booking form, we receive the details you enter: name, email, phone number, date, route preference, guest count and any notes you add. This is used only to respond to your booking request and arrange the ride — nothing else.

Cookies & third-party services

  • Google Fonts — this site loads typefaces from Google's servers. Google may log the request (such as your IP address) under its own privacy policy.
  • Formspree (booking form) — once connected, form submissions are processed by Formspree to deliver them to our inbox. See Formspree's privacy policy.

We do not use advertising or behavioural-tracking cookies, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you can ask us what data we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Contact us at info@partytankprague.com and we'll handle it directly.

Data retention

Booking request details are kept only as long as needed to handle your enquiry and any resulting booking, plus a reasonable period for accounting and legal purposes where applicable.
