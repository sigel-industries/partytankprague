    FAQ

    The practical part.
    Without the small print theatre.

    The essentials before you send a request. Everything specific is confirmed for your date.

    How many people fit inside?

    Up to 13 guests. Tell us the group size and we will confirm the most comfortable setup for the planned ride.

    Can the route leave Prague?

    Yes. Pickup, destination and stops can be planned in Prague or outside it, depending on time and agreement.

    Can drinks or decorations be prepared?

    They can be discussed as part of the request. Exact options, availability and price are confirmed before the booking.

    How far ahead should we ask?

    Earlier is safer for weekends and popular dates, but last-minute availability is always worth checking.

    Is the price per person or per ride?

    The Hummer is booked as a private ride, usually by duration. The final price is confirmed for the whole request, not charged per passenger.
