    FAQ

    Before you ask.

    How many people fit inside?

    Up to 13 guests in the wraparound lounge. Tell us your group size and we'll confirm comfort for the route.

    Can the route leave Prague?

    Yes — the Bohemian Day route runs out toward Karlštejn and the vineyard roads beyond it. Tell us what you'd like to see.

    Can we bring our own drinks?

    The on-board mini-bar is yours for the ride. Bring what you like, we'll keep it cold.

    How far ahead should we book?

    Weekends and stag/hen season fill fast — 2–3 weeks out is safest, but ask about last-minute availability.

    Is the price per person or per ride?

    Per ride, not per person — split across 13 guests it's usually less than a fleet of taxis.

    The car is free most nights.
