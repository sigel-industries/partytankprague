FAQ

Before you ask, we answer.

How many people fit inside?

Up to 16 guests, seated in the wraparound lounge. Tell us your group size and we'll confirm comfort for the route you want.

Can we bring our own drinks?

Yes — the on-board mini-bar is yours for the ride. Bring what you like, we'll keep it cold.

Do you only drive in Prague?

Prague city routes are our specialty, including airport and out-of-town transfers on request.

How far in advance should we book?

Weekends and stag/hen season fill up fast — 2–3 weeks ahead is safest, but ask us about last-minute availability.

Is the price per hour or per person?

Pricing is per ride, not per person — split between 16 guests, it's usually less than a round of taxis.
