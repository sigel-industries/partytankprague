FAQ

Questions before someone overthinks booking a giant limo.

How many people fit inside?

The Hummer limo is suitable for up to 13 guests.

Is it good for weddings?

Yes. It works well for arrival, photo moments, transfer between venues or a stronger wedding-night entrance.

Can we choose the route?

Yes. Add pickup, destination and desired stops in the booking request. The final route is confirmed individually.

Does the form already send requests?

Not in this prototype. It is prepared visually. Connect it later to Formspree, Make, n8n, e-mail or your own backend.
