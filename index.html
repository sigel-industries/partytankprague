    FAQ

    Before you ask.

    The practical things people usually need before sending a request.

    How many people fit inside?

    Up to 13 guests in the wraparound lounge. Tell us your group size and we'll confirm comfort for the route.

    Can the route leave Prague?

    Yes. Pickup, destination and route are agreed according to the occasion and timing.

    Can drinks or decorations be prepared?

    Possible options are agreed before the date. We confirm availability and price before anything is ordered.

    How far ahead should we ask?

    Earlier is safer for weekends and popular dates, but last-minute availability is always worth checking.

    Is the price per person or per ride?

    The service is priced per ride and duration, not per passenger. The final price is confirmed with the route and requested preparation.
